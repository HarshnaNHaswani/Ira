Carnations

Eurasian Native

Carnations are known as the "The Flowers of God". The single flowers of the Carnations species, Dianthus Caryophyllus have 5 petals and vary from white to pink to purple in color. Border Carnation cultivars may have double flowers with as many as 40 petals. They come in numerous colors and each color of carnation has a different meaning. The Carnation leaves are narrow and stalk less and their color varies from green to grey-blue or purple. Carnations grow big, full blooms on strong, straight stems.
